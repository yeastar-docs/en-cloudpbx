Modify Your Profile

  1. At the top-right conner, click your account, and choose Settings.
  2. On the configuration page, modify your profile.
    • Name: Enter your name.
    • Email: Your account name. Unchangeable.
    • Role: Your role of the Remote Management. Unchangeable.
    • Managing/Max Manageable: Your management status and permission.
    • Send alarm to email: If an alarm occurs on your device, you will receive the notification email.
  3. Click Save.
